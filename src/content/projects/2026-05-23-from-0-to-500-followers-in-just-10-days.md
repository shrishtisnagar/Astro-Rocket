**Platform: LinkedIn**

**Account type: Personal**

## **PROBLEM:**

In Feb 2023, the Founder of a startup media company reached out to me for marketing services. He wanted to establish a Personal Brand on LinkedIn, which meant a 180° turn from his current industry to media services. He had just started the account which posed further problems.

## **OBJECTIVE:**

He wanted to achieve two things:

1. Grow the account, i.e., increase the follower count.
2. Brand awareness, he wanted people to know that the company exists in the industry and what it is up to.

## **MY APPROACH:**

I spent a week researching the industry and competitors to better understand the company and its business. I also worked with the founder to discover his personal style and USP.

After that, I optimized his profile in the second week of February, which increased search appearances.

In the same week, I created a content creation and distribution strategy for his profile. I planned to post 3 times a week. In addition to this, I actively worked on expanding his network and engaging with the community.

In just 10 days, his follower count grew from **0 to 500+**

His post impressions also went from **25 to 1771**
