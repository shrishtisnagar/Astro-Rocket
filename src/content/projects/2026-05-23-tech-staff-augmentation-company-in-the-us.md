**Platform: LinkedIn**

**Account type: Company**

## **PROBLEM:**

This was a continuation of a personal branding project - where we also realised the company’s digital presence didn’t reflect the quality of their work.

The company page wasn’t bad, but the branding efforts weren’t cohesive. The founder wanted a steady presence that looked credible, without burning internal resources.

## **OBJECTIVE:**

- Build a consistent, professional company brand on LinkedIn
- Post regularly without needing a full-time social media hire
- Improve engagement + visibility with low effort from the client

## **MY APPROACH:**

I started from scratch - audited the existing page, analyzed competitors, researched buyer language, and interviewed the founder to get clarity on what really made them different.

I rewrote the About section to clarify their niche, cleaned up all visuals, and designed a monthly post calendar with the following pillars:

• Industry-specific red flags • Hiring trends in the US • Demand gen for staff augmentation

I also optimized post tags, timing, and interactions to drive better reach - without relying on paid boosts.

We also worked on the personal brand of the founder to get more visibility. [Entire case study here.](https://www.notion.so/Inbound-Outbound-Funnel-for-Tech-Staff-Augmentation-Founder-2391d962da4580e9bb5ef51bc2f4e53b?pvs=21)
