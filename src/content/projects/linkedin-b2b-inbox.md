**Platform: LinkedIn**

**Account type: Personal**

## **PROBLEM:**

In Sept 2023, something different happened. The CEO of a very niche mining-related startup approached me. He had been consistent on LinkedIn but failed to get traction that would get attention from his potential customers.

## **OBJECTIVE:**

He wanted to achieve two things:

1. Be known as a thought leader in his unique niche domain
2. Nurture relationships with potential customers organically

## **MY APPROACH:**

As always, this project started with a good old thorough research. Especially because this domain was so unique and kind of an unknown territory to me.

I studied his target audience and did competitor research and market analysis on LinkedIn.

I also studied his website and products + services

I also gave his LinkedIn presence (Profile + company page) a completely new makeover

Once all things were in place, we proceeded to the STRATEGIES!

I knew this had to be a balanced project.

The classic “talk about product features” approach wasn’t going to work for him. I decided to add different layers to how we were going to approach the content.

So I conquered this in 3 ways:

a) Awareness-oriented content to target miners and their pain points

b) Educational Content regarding myths/truths about his services

c) Personal content to establish thought leadership

We started the implementation of the strategy on 1st September

## **We got these stats for his LinkedIn Account in a Month:**
