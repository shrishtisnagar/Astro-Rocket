**Platform: LinkedIn**

**Account type: Personal**

# **PROBLEM:**

In Aug 2023, a mindset & life coach from Florida, US, approached me for my personal branding services. She successfully ran her practice and already had a presence on Instagram, but was looking for more exposure on LinkedIn, which was a brand-new territory for her.

# **OBJECTIVE:**

She wanted to achieve two things:

1. Get more exposure from LinkedIn as a platform
2. Get more registrations for her signature “Mindset-Monday” Program

# **MY APPROACH:**

I started this project with a good old thorough research.

I studied her target audience, did competitor research and market analysis on LinkedIn, and offered her resources to nail down her personal brand.

I also studied her existing presence on Instagram and her website.

Before starting, I reviewed her existing funnel for her signature program on Eventbrite.

It lacked the charm, and I therefore decided to give it a makeover.

I also gave her LinkedIn presence (Profile + company page) a completely new makeover

Once all things were in place, we proceeded to the STRATEGIES!

I knew this had to be a MOFU+BOFU heavy project

So I conquered this in 3 ways:

a) Reactivating the current network + Expanding network

b) Content Strategy to focus on Workshop Signups

c) LinkedIn event strategies to make sure the funnel is right

We started the implementation of the strategy on 1st September
